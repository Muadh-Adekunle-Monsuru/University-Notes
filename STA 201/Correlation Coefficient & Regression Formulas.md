$$\large{r= \frac{nΣxy - ΣxΣy }{\sqrt{[nΣx^2 - (Σx )^2][nΣy^2 - (Σy )^2]}}}$$
## &
$$\large{r=\frac{Σ(x-x̄)(y-ȳ) }{\sqrt{Σ(x-x̄)^2(y-ȳ)^2}}}$$

## Spearman Rank correlation coefficient
$$\large{r= 1- \frac{6Σdi^2}{n(n^2-1)}}$$

$$\large{di=R_x - R_y}$$
$R_x$ is Rank Of X While $R_y$ is Rank of Y
